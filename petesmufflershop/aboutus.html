History of Pete's Muffler Shop

This Muffler shop has been owned and operated by the Ruiz family for three generations. It first opened it's doors on May 1983 with Pedro "Pete" Ruiz at the helm. Pedro Ruiz later handed over the bussiness to his son Homero Ruiz, who has been running Pete's Muffler Shop to this day. Homero Ruiz began expanding his bussiness in 1994 thru 2018 by purchasing additional properties and expanding the building. The Ruiz family takes great pride in knowing that their bussiness has grown allong side the community of La Joya, Texas. Pete's Muffler Shop routinely sponsers childeren, students, and young teens in a vast array of althletic, dance, academic, and many other extracurricular ativities in and outside of school. Pete's Muffler Shop annually participates in the city of La Joya seasonal activities for the community such as Halloween Bash, National Night Out, Christmas Parade. Most recently Pete's Muffler Shop was a gold sponsor for Dutches of Retama Pagent


Currently this Homero Ruiz is the owner and operator of this establishment. He inherited the bussiness from his father Pedro "PETE" Ruiz. Homero Ruiz is poised to retire soon and has three childeren that are eager to keep the their family legacy alive.
