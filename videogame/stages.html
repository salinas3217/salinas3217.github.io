First 5 Cities in the Game

Peoria, Illinois
Description of Level: A Pickup Truck is slowly moving from right to left across the bottom of the screen, and the second building has a neon sign for "DRUG" upon its right side
Joliet, Illinois
Description of Level: A Taxi is parked on the right side at the bottom of the screen, and if it is left for long enough, it shall start up, and drive away off the left side of the screen
Chicago, Illinois
Description of Level: Because the ground has rails on it, a Train may come into view from either side of the screen during the day; the sixth building is also named as "BANK"
Chicago, Illinois
Description of Level: A Taxi is parked on the right side at the bottom of the screen, and if it is left for long enough, it shall start up, and drive away off the left side of the screen; the third building also has a neon sign for "COLD BEER" upon its left side, and a US National Guard Tank will drive into view from either side of the screen
Kalamazoo, Michigan
Description of Level: A Taxi is slowly moving from right to left across the bottom of the screen, and the third building has a neon sign for "EATS" upon its right side; there is also a manhole cover on the ground, which shall fly up into the air when it is punched (and destroy any US National Guard Helicopter it comes down on top of)
